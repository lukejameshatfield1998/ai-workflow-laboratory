## What this helps with

This tutorial helps you turn rough notes into a professional customer email.

## Step 1: Write rough notes

Example:

> Freezer fault. Evap fan motor failed. Replaced. Freezer now okay.

## Step 2: Ask AI to rewrite it

Use this prompt:

> Write a professional customer update. The fault was a failed evaporator fan motor. The part has been replaced and the freezer is operating normally.

## Step 3: Check the result

Make sure the AI has not invented anything.
