## Introduction

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) reduce support requests and help customers find answers quickly.

AI can generate a comprehensive FAQ section from basic information about your products or services.

## Step 1: Describe Your Business

Example:

"We provide commercial refrigeration maintenance, repairs, and emergency breakdown cover."

## Step 2: Generate FAQ Questions

Prompt:

"Generate 25 common customer questions and answers for a commercial refrigeration company."

## Step 3: Refine the Responses

Review answers to ensure accuracy and compliance with company policies.

## Example Questions

Q: Do you provide emergency call-outs?

A: Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency breakdown support.

Q: How quickly can an engineer attend?

A: Response times vary by location and contract arrangements.

Q: Do you carry spare parts?

A: Engineers carry commonly used parts and can source specialist components if required.

## Final Thoughts

A well-designed FAQ section can improve customer experience and reduce support costs.
