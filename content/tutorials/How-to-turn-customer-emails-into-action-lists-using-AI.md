## Introduction

Important tasks can easily be missed when dealing with large volumes of customer emails. AI can quickly identify actions, priorities, and responsibilities.

## Step 1: Copy the Email

Paste the customer's email into your AI tool.

## Step 2: Create an Action List

Prompt:

"Analyse this customer email and create a prioritised action list including urgency level, required actions, and recommended next steps."

## Step 3: Review the Output

Ensure all important tasks have been captured.

## Example Output

Urgency Level:
High

Required Actions:

* Contact customer immediately.
* Dispatch engineer.
* Verify equipment temperatures.
* Arrange replacement parts if required.

Recommended Priority:
Immediate response within one hour.

## Final Thoughts

AI can help businesses respond faster and ensure critical tasks are not overlooked.
