## Introduction

Many businesses spend hours each week writing meeting notes and action plans. AI can transform rough notes into professional summaries within seconds.

## Step 1: Gather Your Notes

Copy your notes into ChatGPT.

Example:

* Freezer breakdown at Store 25
* Engineer dispatched
* Replacement fan motor required
* Customer requested daily updates
* Parts arriving tomorrow

## Step 2: Ask AI to Create a Summary

Prompt:

"Convert these meeting notes into professional meeting minutes with action items."

## Step 3: Generate Action Lists

You can also ask:

"Create a list of actions, responsible people, and deadlines."

## Example Output

Meeting Summary:

A discussion was held regarding the refrigeration breakdown at Store 25. An engineer has been dispatched and identified a failed evaporator fan motor. Replacement parts have been ordered and are expected to arrive tomorrow.

Action Items:

* Engineering Team: Install replacement motor.
* Operations Team: Provide daily customer updates.
* Stores Team: Monitor product temperatures.

## Final Thoughts

Using AI for meeting summaries ensures important information is captured consistently while reducing administration time.
