## Introduction

Professional quotations help build trust and increase the likelihood of winning business. AI can quickly generate quotation drafts that can then be reviewed and customised.

## Step 1: Gather Project Information

Provide:

* Scope of work
* Labour costs
* Parts costs
* Call-out charges
* Additional notes

## Step 2: Ask AI to Create the Quote

Prompt:

"Create a professional quotation for replacing a faulty evaporator fan motor in a supermarket cold room. Include labour, parts, call-out charge, scope of work, and terms."

## Step 3: Review Before Sending

Verify pricing, scope, and customer details.

## Example Output

Quotation Summary

Scope of Work:
Supply and replace failed evaporator fan motor.

Labour:
£250

Parts:
£180

Call-Out Charge:
£95

Total:
£525

Terms:
All work to be completed during normal working hours unless otherwise agreed.

## Final Thoughts

AI helps create consistent, professional quotations while reducing administrative workload.
