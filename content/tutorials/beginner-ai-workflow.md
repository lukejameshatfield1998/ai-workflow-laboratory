## The workflow

1. Write messy notes
2. Ask AI to organise them
3. Ask AI to turn them into a checklist
4. Review the result
5. Save the final version as a reusable template

## Example prompt

> Turn these notes into a clear checklist for a small business owner. Keep it simple and practical.

## Why it works

Most people do not need complicated automation straight away. They need repeatable systems that save time.
