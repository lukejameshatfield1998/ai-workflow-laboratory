## Introduction

Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) help businesses maintain consistency, improve training, and reduce mistakes. Creating SOPs manually can be time-consuming, but AI can help generate professional procedures in minutes.

## Step 1: Describe the Process

Provide AI with details of the task.

Example:

"Our engineers perform a monthly refrigeration maintenance visit. The process includes checking temperatures, inspecting condensers, cleaning coils, checking refrigerant levels, and completing a report."

## Step 2: Ask AI to Create an SOP

Prompt:

"Create a professional Standard Operating Procedure for this process including objectives, responsibilities, procedure steps, and documentation requirements."

## Step 3: Review and Customise

Ensure the procedure reflects your company's processes and compliance requirements.

## Example Output

Monthly Refrigeration Maintenance SOP

Objective:
Ensure refrigeration equipment operates efficiently and reliably.

Procedure:

1. Arrive on site and sign in.
2. Inspect equipment condition.
3. Check operating temperatures.
4. Clean condenser coils if required.
5. Verify refrigerant pressures.
6. Record findings.
7. Complete maintenance report.

## Final Thoughts

AI can significantly reduce the time required to document business processes while improving consistency across teams.
